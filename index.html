Quick Installation Guide
1

Download & Extract

Click the download button above to get the markhor-voice-messenger.zip file, and extract its contents to a folder on your computer.

2

Open Extensions Manager

Open Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/ (or open Chrome Menu → Extensions → Manage Extensions).

3

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle the Developer mode switch in the top-right corner of the extensions page to ON.

4

Load Unpacked Folder

Click the Load unpacked button in the top-left corner, and select the extracted extension folder. Your extension is ready!
